What is INESData?

An incubator of data spaces and AI services at the national level using federated infrastructures in the Cloud.

We help align the European Data Strategy with data-driven startups and SMEs by facilitating the development of reliable and secure innovative solutions based on industrial and personal data.

Infrastructure for your Data Space

We provide infrastructure and components for the creation of data spaces aligned with proposals at the European level (IDSA, Gaia-X).

Artificial Intelligence Services

We convert data into knowledge through the availability of multimodal AI services - Language Processing, Knowledge Graphs and multimedia analysis.

Enable Data Spaces for different verticals

We enable a series of industry-based Data Spaces in Spain: linguistic, legal and public procurement, mobility and media sectors
